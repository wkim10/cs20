Our Story

Our story began when a group of amateur astronomers realized that there was a need for reliable and expert telescope repair services. We saw telescopes languishing in closets and attics, collecting dust because their owners had nowhere to turn for repairs. We decided to change that.

With years of experience in the field, we founded Expert Telescope Repairs, a place where fellow astronomy enthusiasts could bring their beloved telescopes for precision care and restoration. From the very start, our focus has been on delivering top-quality service, exceptional craftsmanship, and a genuine love for the cosmos.
